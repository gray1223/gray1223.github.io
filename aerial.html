Professional Real Estate Photography Services

Why Choose GAV?

Gray AerialVisual has been doing real estate photography since 2015. GAV holds an FAA Part 107 Certificate and liability insurance for all aerial work. With over two dozen successful shoots and 5.0 stars on Google Reviews, you can trust GAV to deliver quality content on a timeline.

What's the turnaround time?

GAV will deliver all still assets within 12 hours of shooting. Video, portfolios, and other time-intensive assets may take 1-3 days for delivery.
